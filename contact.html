🙏 Visitor Information

👗

Dress Code

Traditional or modest clothing preferred. Avoid sleeveless tops and short clothes as a mark of respect.

🤫

Ashram Etiquette

Maintain silence and peace within the ashram premises. Switch off mobile phones. Photography only in permitted areas.

🏠

Accommodation

Guest rooms may be available for devotees. Please contact the ashram office in advance for availability and booking.

🍽

Prasad & Meals

Free prasad (sacred food) is distributed at the ashram. Meals may be available on festival days and special occasions.

Sacred Calendar

Annual Events & Celebrations

🎂

Jayanti — Birthday Celebration

December 1st every year — Grand celebration of Swamiji's birthday with puja, bhajans, and discourse. Thousands of devotees attend.

🙏

Aradhana — Mahasamadhi Day

February 20th — Sacred day commemorating Swamiji's Mahasamadhi. Special puja and prayers are conducted at the Samadhi Mandir.

🌙

Monthly Puja Days

Special pujas on Ekadashi, Pournami (full moon), and Amavasai (new moon) days every month. Check with the ashram for schedule.

🔥

Karthigai Deepam

The sacred festival of lights at Tiruvannamalai — celebrated with special significance at the ashram and at the holy Arunachala hill.
